How long until I see results?
+

Your core flows will be live within 20 days of kickoff. Revenue from automated flows typically starts accruing within the first week they're active. Campaign results appear from your first monthly send. Most clients see meaningful lift within the first 30–45 days.

What if I already have flows set up?
+

The Klaviyo audit in week one will tell us exactly which flows are worth keeping (with improvements) and which ones need to be rebuilt. Most existing flows have fundamental issues — wrong triggers, poor segmentation, weak copy, outdated design. We fix what's broken and rebuild what's holding you back.

+

I work with DTC Shopify brands doing $100K or more per month in revenue. At that scale, email is a serious lever — and the ROI of getting it right is substantial. Below that, the economics of a full-service retainer don't always make sense, and I'll tell you that honestly on the call.

No. That's the whole point. You approve the monthly calendar and review performance reports. Everything else — strategy, copy, design, technical setup, sends, segmentation — is handled by my team. Communication happens async via Slack or email, no mandatory weekly calls.

What's included in the retainer?

Full strategy, all copywriting, email design, Klaviyo technical setup, automation flows, campaign execution, segmentation management, SMS strategy, A/B testing, and monthly reporting. Done for you, entirely. You own the Klaviyo account — we just run it.

How long is the contract?
+

Month-to-month after the initial onboarding period. I don't lock clients into long contracts because I believe results should speak for themselves. If you're not seeing revenue move, you shouldn't be paying for it. Most clients stay for 12+ months because the numbers keep improving.
