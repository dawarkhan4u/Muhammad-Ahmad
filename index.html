BEFORE: Average results. Average open rates. No segmentation strategy. Sending to the full list, watching engagement slowly decay. AFTER: 60.4% open rate. 4/4 Excellent ratings across every metric. Industry average is 25–35%.

THE SITUATION

An eCommerce brand with a solid product, a healthy list, and results that were average at best. Open rates sat in the mid-twenties — technically not bad, but not indicative of an engaged audience. The problem wasn't the product, and it wasn't the list — it was the strategy. Every campaign went to everyone, regardless of engagement level, purchase history, or where they were in the customer journey. The list was slowly burning out.

WHAT WE DID

We started with a full list audit before sending a single campaign. Cleaned the list first — removed unengageable contacts, sunset inactive subscribers, and rebuilt segments from the ground up. Only after the list was clean did I start sending — and when I did, every campaign went to a defined segment, not the full list. We ran 7 campaigns in 30 days — not carpet-bombing, but precision sends that built momentum.

THE OUTCOME

60.4% open rate — nearly double the industry average. 4 out of 4 Excellent ratings on Klaviyo's health dashboard, across deliverability, engagement, growth, and compliance. The list didn't just perform better; it became an asset again.
